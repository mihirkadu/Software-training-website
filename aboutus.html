Founded in Year-2000, MAK Technology is a trusted software training institute based in Navi Mumbai , dedicated to helping students and professionals build successful careers in the IT industry. At MAK Technology , we believe that true learning happens through practice. Our programs are designed to deliver hands-on, project-based training guided by experienced industry professionals. We specialize in Full Stack Development, .NET, Java, Python, Web Design, Data analysis and Database Management,Software testing , ensuring every learner gains both technical and practical knowledge. With small batch sizes, personalized mentorship, and flexible timing options, we provide a learning environment that supports growth and confidence. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands by preparing students with job-ready skills, real-world projects, and placement assistance. For over two decades, MAK Technology has been shaping aspiring developers into skilled professionals who succeed in top IT companies across India. Join us and take your first step toward a rewarding career in technology.
